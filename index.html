Privacy Policy for Daily Dare

Last updated: July 2025

Daily Dare does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. All app data—such as your current dare, timers, and settings—is kept locally on your device.

  • No data is ever sent to any server.
  • No analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs are used.
  • Local push notifications are generated on‑device only; no notification tokens are shared.
  • Children’s privacy is fully protected—no personal data is collected, including from minors.
  • Because no external data is stored or transmitted, there is no risk of external data breach.

We may update this policy occasionally; the “Last updated” date will reflect the current version.

For questions, please contact us at privacy@daily-dare.app.
